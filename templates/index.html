What is PicoShare?

PicoShare is a service for sharing images, videos, and other files.

Unlike other services, PicoShare never:

  • Requires users to sign in to access links
  • Resizes or re-encodes images or videos
  • Discards original filenames

PicoShare is easy to self-host. See our source repo for details.

PicoShare is written by Michael Lynch and licensed under the open-source AGPLv3 license.
